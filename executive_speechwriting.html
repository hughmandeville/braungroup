Executive Speechwriting

For over a decade, The Braun Group has provided speechwriting services for business executives, crafting speeches that are highly personal and capture each executive’s authentic voice.

We specialize in remarks ranging from introductions and elevator pitches to keynote addresses and commencement speeches. We help executives deliver memorable messages by including select facts, engaging stories, and (when appropriate) humor.

Our team of researchers, writers, and editors provides speeches in various formats—full-text, talking points, notes, or presentations. We are always happy to work with larger teams that include event production companies and corporate marketing groups.
